She Codes Plus

The Plus course is a collaboration between She Codes and BHP to provide an alternate pathway for women to enter tech careers. Participants undertake four unique projects focussed on building an enterprise web application using HTML/CSS, Python, Django and React JS. With over 250 applications for the program, I was lucky enough to gain one of the 25 positions available in the 2019 program!
